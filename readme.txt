Manages information on what food is served daily in a restaurant. Also includes a week-view with a full view of the selected weeks meal choices.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `luncheon` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Place `<?php do_action('luncheon_print_todays_menu'); ?>` in your templates
